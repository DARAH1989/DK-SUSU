FAQs

If you have questions, check here first!

1. What is Susu Savings?

Susu Savings is a traditional form of savings in many African communities, where individuals contribute regularly to a pooled fund for collective financial benefits.

2. How secure is BEMAT Susu Savings?

Your funds are fully secure with us. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your savings.

3. Can I withdraw my savings at any time?

Yes, our flexible savings plans allow you to withdraw at designated intervals or when needed.

4. What are the benefits of joining?

By joining, you gain access to reliable savings plans, community support, and exclusive member benefits.

5. How do I become a member?

Visit our Membership page to sign up and choose a savings plan that suits you.
